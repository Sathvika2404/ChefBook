Chicken Tikka Masala Preparation

Preparation Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, lemon juice, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and grill until cooked through.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree, spices, and cream to the pan and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the grilled chicken pieces to the sauce and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
