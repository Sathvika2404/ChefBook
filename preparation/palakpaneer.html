Palak Paneer Preparation

Preparation Steps

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2 minutes, then drain and transfer to cold water.
  2. Blend blanched spinach leaves to a smooth puree.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and green chilies.
  4. Saut√© until onions are golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  6. Add spinach puree, salt, and spices, and cook until the mixture thickens.
  7. Add cubed paneer pieces and cook for a few minutes.
  8. Finish with a drizzle of cream and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  9. Serve hot with roti or rice.
