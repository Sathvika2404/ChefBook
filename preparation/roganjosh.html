Rogan Josh Preparation

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add whole spices like cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, and other spices.
  5. Add cubed lamb pieces and cook until the meat is browned.
  6. Add water and let it simmer until the meat is tender.
  7. Finish with a drizzle of ghee and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan.
