Butter Chicken Preparation

Preparation Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  2. Grill or roast the marinated chicken until cooked.
  3. Heat butter in a pan, add chopped onions, ginger, and garlic. Saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, and other spices. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add the cooked chicken pieces to the sauce. Simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Finish with a drizzle of cream and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
