Chole Preparation

Preparation Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas in water overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  2. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with tea bags, bay leaf, and salt until soft.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and green chilies.
  4. Saut√© until onions are golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  6. Add boiled chickpeas along with water, salt, and spices, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with bhature or rice.
