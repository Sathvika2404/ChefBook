Dal Makhani Preparation

Preparation Steps

  1. Soak whole black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma) in water overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  2. Pressure cook soaked lentils and beans with water, salt, and a pinch of turmeric until soft and well-cooked.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and green chilies.
  4. Saut√© until onions are translucent, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  6. Add cooked lentils and beans along with water, salt, and spices, and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  7. Mash some of the lentils and beans with the back of a spoon to thicken the dal.
  8. Finish with a drizzle of cream and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  9. Serve hot with rice or naan.
